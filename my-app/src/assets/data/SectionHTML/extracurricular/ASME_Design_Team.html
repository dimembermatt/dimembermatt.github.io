The ASME Design Team, formerly known as the Rube Goldberg team, is a fun group that participates in various activities from Case Competitions, to making a commercial with Reliant Energy, to trekking out to Purdue University and winning third place in the National Chain Reaction Competition with our Rube Goldberg machine.

As a member of this org, I have fun de-stressing from high-pace environment of my other responsibilities, putting my creative skills to the test by building engaging low-tech contraptions.

I'm less active than I used to be, but I have fun showing up sporadically at their build meetings to help out.
