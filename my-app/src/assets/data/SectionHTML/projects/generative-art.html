Summer 2018 - Present

Previously what was a couple day project at the end of July 2018, this project has become my longest lasting (besides the redevelopment of my personal site) and most fruitful.

It started out after the work with the Web Audio Visualizer project described above, in which I was intrigued by generative art and wanted to make my own programs that created designs based off of mathematical or natural phenomenon.

Since then, I've churned out at maybe about a dozen small and large programs to generate art; and have about two dozen more sketches in a notebook I carry around. There are a lot more ideas I want to and plan to implement in the future.

I am planning on updating that page by the end of summer with the programs that were previously on the old projects page and with some OpenFrameworks programs that I have not yet published.
